Ever felt that sinking feeling in your stomach that you're never going to see your deposit again?

Deposits suck. They really do. Giving away your hard-earned money to a stranger on the internet is far from ideal, no matter how beautiful that house. But it doesn't have to be this way. SafeDeposit acts as a trusted intermediary, safeguarding both your and your depositor's interests - safeguarding your money, while also providing your landlord or depositor a guarantee of money deposited. We do all this on a public ledger, ensuring complete transparency at all times. With our propreitary dispute resolution process, we aim for 100% satisfaction on all fronts. Your deposit is always returned to you within 21 days of the end of your contract if no claims are filed. And landlords - its time to do away with the headache of managing tenant deposits and all the troubles that come with it.

In the biz, this is what we call a win-win situation.

How It Works

Step 1 - Make the Drop

Venmo the SafeDeposit Ledger @SafeDeposit with your safety deposit. Ensure that you make the payment public and mention your landlord/depositor in the comments along with the purpose and end date of the deposit. If you'd like us to intimate the recipient of your deposit, add their contact details as well.

Step 2 - We Safeguard your Deposit

When you move in, just send us pictures of how the place looks. This is to safeguard both parties. Rest in peace as we hold onto your deposit while you enjoy your stay or do whatever it is that required that deposit.

Step 3- Get back that dough

When you move out, send us pictures again. 21 days after the end of your contract, your money is returned to you automatically if no claims are filed by the depositor by then. No stress, no drama. No 600 calls and texts and follow-ups to get your money back.

*For Landlords and Depositors* In case shit hits the fan

Some people are arseholes, we get it. If everything was perfect, you wouldn't need deposits in the first place. In case your tenant actually trashed your place, or violated a predetermined contract, just shoot us an email

With our propreitary dispute resolution mechanisms, we'll resolve the issue. Make sure you file your claim within 21 days of the end date - that's the deposit holding period.
